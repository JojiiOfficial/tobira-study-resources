Tobira Exercises

Welcome to Tobira Study Resources! The exercises provided here are for use with Tobira: Gateway to Advanced Japanese textbooks and are meant to help you practice what you have learned in each lesson. Select a lesson from the quick navigation and then the exercise that you want to practice for that lesson to begin testing your knowledge. Happy studying!
